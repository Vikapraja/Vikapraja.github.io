Marquee Tag

=>It is used to make move the contents whith are in marquee tag. 
            #Attribute:
                1)behaviour="alternate/slide/scroll"
                2)direction="left/right/top/down"  
                3)scrollamount="(int)px"  [for speed of move]
                4)alt="..text.."
                5)width="(int)px"
                6)height="(int)px"
