#Padding And Margin in CSS

  • Padding=>Padding is used to create space around an elment's content, inside of any defined border.
  • Margin=>Margin are used to create space around element,outside of any defined borders.

#NOTE :

  1. Padding are inside element
  2. Margin are outside of element
